Women continue to be underrepresented in male-dominated industries (business, the natural sciences, and engineering). De-genderization of wording is one way in which to minimize the perpetuation of gender-based inequalities in these fields (Gaucher, Friesen, Kay 2011).

To help minimize implicit gender bias, analyze the tone of your content to improve its effectiveness. Use the results to ensure your writing comes across with the social tone and emotional impact intended.
